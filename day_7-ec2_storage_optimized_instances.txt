Storage Optimized Instances  : "sequential READ and WRITE"
============================ 
--> sequential --> one after another
--> It is used for "sequential READ and WRITE" access
--> Storage Optimized Instances are design  for work loads that require high, "sequential READ and WRITE" access
	to be very large data set on local storage.
	example
	=======
--> youtube --> user can upload the data , share the data and read the data
--> music and video web site

they are three types of Storage Optimized Instances
---------------------------------------------------
1: D - series --> D2 instance --> menimum capacity
2: H - series --> H1 instance --> medium capacity
3: I - series --> I3 and I3en instances --> large capacity
